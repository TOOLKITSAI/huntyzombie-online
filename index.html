Game Introduction

Core Features & Systems

Become the Ultimate Zombie Hunter in this Anime Wave Defense Experience

Hunty Zombie is an anime-style wave defense Roblox game where YOU are the predator, not the prey. Fight waves of zombies across multiple maps in solo or co-op mode, collect 6 unique weapons through the Gacha system (press J for Normal Spins at $3,000 each), and equip up to 2 pets from a roster of 10 collectible companions. Features Normal and Nightmare difficulty modes with TNT zombies. Currently in UPDATE 2 (August 28, 2025) - developed by HZ Dev team.

Wave Defense Combat

Survive endless waves with strategic positioning and timing. Each round brings stronger, more diverse zombie types requiring adaptive tactics.

Progressive Difficulty

Dynamic scaling system ensures perfect challenge balance. Face increasingly challenging zombie waves with special enemy types including TNT zombies in Nightmare mode.

Multiplayer Co-op

Team up with friends for coordinated survival strategies. Share resources and combine abilities for maximum effectiveness.

Weapon Trait System

Enhance your weapons with powerful traits using Trait Crystals obtained from gameplay or secret quests from the unmarked NPC (left of Weapons NPC in lobby). Each weapon can have ONE trait:

  • Prodigy (1.5%): +15% damage, +6% speed, +15% skill damage
  • Beast (2.5%): +10% damage, +2% attack speed
  • Fortune (4%): +20% gold drop chance
  • Power III (4%): +15% damage
  • Agility III (4%): +6% attack speed
  • Focus III (4%): +15% skill damage

Pro Tip: Wait for Legendary/Mythic weapons before using Trait Crystals. Prodigy is the ultimate end-game trait!
