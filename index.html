Weapon Trait System

Enhance your weapons with powerful traits using Trait Crystals obtained from gameplay or secret quests from the unmarked NPC (left of Weapons NPC in lobby). Each weapon can have ONE trait:

  • Prodigy (1.5%): +15% damage, +6% speed, +15% skill damage
  • Beast (2.5%): +10% damage, +2% attack speed
  • Fortune (4%): +20% gold drop chance
  • Power III (4%): +15% damage
  • Agility III (4%): +6% attack speed
  • Focus III (4%): +15% skill damage

Pro Tip: Wait for Legendary/Mythic weapons before using Trait Crystals. Prodigy is the ultimate end-game trait!

Frequently Asked Questions

Community Q&A

Getting Started

What's the best starter weapon to aim for?

Your first goal should be getting any Epic weapon (preferably Axes) from the gacha. This will carry you through early waves much better than the starter weapon. Save 3,000 cash and do a normal spin - if you get Axes or better, you're set for early game!

Should I use normal or lucky spins?

Early game: Use normal spins ($3,000) to replace starter weapon. Mid-late game: Save Lucky Spins (obtained from codes and daily rewards) as they significantly increase chances for Legendary/Mythic items with better drop rates.

How do codes work and where do I redeem them?

Codes can ONLY be redeemed in the game lobby (not during waves). Click the CODES button on the right side of your screen, type the code exactly as shown (case-sensitive), and click Confirm. Each code can only be used once per account.

Combat & Strategy

What are i-frames and how do I use them?

I-frames (invincibility frames) make you immune to damage briefly. Key sources: Dodge roll (Q key) and certain weapon abilities provide invincibility windows. Chain these strategically to avoid damage during dangerous situations!

How does the perk charging system work?

Your perk (E ability) charges by killing zombies. Each kill adds charge, with elite zombies giving more. Once fully charged, press E to activate. Vampire and Critical are considered the best perks due to their powerful effects.

What's the best strategy for boss rounds?

Save your ultimate (G) and perk (E) for tough waves. Kite zombies in circles while using ranged attacks, dodge special zombie attacks with Q-roll, and burst them down when grouped together. Extra caution needed for TNT zombies in Nightmare mode!

Economy & Progression

What's the most efficient way to farm cash?

Join multiplayer lobbies for bonus cash, equip Raven pet for +30% drops, use Fortune trait for +20% gold chance, and focus on reaching wave 50+ consistently. Events with 2x cash are the absolute best farming times.

When should I start investing in traits?

Wait until you have a Legendary or Mythic weapon you'll keep long-term. Trait crystals drop from enemies or secret quests from the unmarked NPC. Don't waste them on weapons you'll replace. Aim for Power III, Agility III, or the ultimate Prodigy trait (+15% damage, +6% speed, +15% skill damage).

Is it worth buying Robux items?

The game is fully playable F2P. Robux purchases mainly save time - they don't give exclusive power. The best value Robux purchases are lucky spin bundles during events, if you choose to spend.
