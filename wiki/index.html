Weapons Arsenal

Weapons are your primary tools for surviving endless zombie waves in Hunty Zombie. Developed by HZ DEV, this popular Roblox wave survival game has over 163 million visits and features 4 weapon rarity tiers. Each weapon has 3 basic abilities (Z, X, C keys) that charge over time, plus an ultimate ability (G key). Pro Tip: Unlock the second weapon slot for $50,000 to switch weapons during waves using keys 1-2.

How to Obtain Weapons

Weapons are obtained through the Gacha system (press J to open). Normal Spins cost $3,000 each, while Lucky Spins (obtained from codes and daily rewards) significantly increase your chances of getting Legendary and Mythic weapons.

Current Meta (UPDATE 2): Greatsword, Scythe, and Dagger are the top-tier weapons with excellent AoE damage and stagger capabilities. Pair any of these with Prodigy trait, Vampire perk, and Dragon/Raven pets for optimal wave farming.

Perks & Abilities

Perks are special abilities that enhance your performance in combat. Activate your equipped perk by pressing 'E' after charging it up through zombie kills. Each perk provides unique advantages, from damage boosts to healing effects.

Weapon Traits

Traits are powerful enhancements that can be applied to weapons. Each weapon can only have one trait at a time. Use Trait Crystals at the Trait NPC in the lobby to reroll traits on your weapons.

Secret Quests: Visit the unmarked NPC to the left of the Weapons NPC in the lobby. Quests refresh every 24 hours and typically require zombie kills or rounds survived for Trait Crystal rewards.

Wave Progression System

Each map features progressive waves with scaling difficulty. Enemy health, damage, and spawn rates increase with each wave. Pro Strategy: Save your ultimate abilities (G key) and charged perks (E key) for later waves when zombie density peaks. Death resets your current wave progress but you retain all permanent upgrades, coins, and weapon unlocks.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get better weapons?
Weapons are obtained through the Gacha system (press J to open). Normal Spins cost $3,000 while Lucky Spins give better chances at Mythic weapons. Pro Tip: Unlock the second weapon slot for $50,000 to switch weapons mid-game using keys 1-2.
What are the best pets for beginners?
For beginners, focus on pets that increase survivability. The Pig (+10% HP) from the Legendary Pack is excellent for staying alive. Meta Choice: Dragon (damage) and Raven (farming) are the best pets for experienced players pushing high waves.
How do I reroll weapon traits?
Visit the Trait NPC in the lobby with a Trait Crystal. Select the weapon you want to enhance and click the Reroll button. Trait Crystals can be obtained from enemy drops, secret quests, or the Robux shop.
What's the difference between difficulty modes?
Normal mode is for learning the basics with standard enemy stats. Hard mode increases enemy health and damage by 50% but offers better rewards. Nightmare mode doubles enemy stats and spawn rates but provides the best rewards and exclusive items - not recommended for beginners.
How do I redeem codes in the game?
Codes can only be redeemed in the lobby. Click the CODES button on the right side of your screen, enter the code exactly as shown (they're case-sensitive), and click Confirm to receive your rewards. Check our codes page for the latest active codes.
Can I trade weapons or pets with other players?
Currently, Hunty Zombie does not support trading between players. All weapons and pets must be obtained through the game's systems - Gacha for weapons and Pet Packs for pets. Focus on building your own collection through gameplay.
What's the current meta build?
UPDATE 2 Meta: Greatsword or Scythe with Prodigy trait, paired with Vampire perk, and Dragon + Raven pets. The Dagger (limited quest weapon) is also S-tier if you can unlock it. This setup dominates wave farming and high-level progression.
How do Secret Quests work?
Secret Quests are obtained from the unmarked NPC to the left of the Weapons NPC in the lobby. These quests refresh every 24 hours and typically require zombie kills or surviving rounds. Rewards include Trait Crystals for rerolling weapon traits - essential for getting Prodigy trait.
