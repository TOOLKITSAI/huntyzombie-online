Frequently Asked Questions

The Sword is hands-down the best starter weapon for new players. At just 1,000 coins, it offers excellent value with decent damage output and a manageable attack speed. Focus on upgrading it to level 3 first before investing in other weapons. Once you reach 50,000 coins, consider transitioning to the Axe for better crowd control, or save up for the Greatsword if you prefer high single-target damage. Remember to always maintain at least 10,000 coins as emergency reserve for armor repairs and revives!

Nightmare Mode unlocks after reaching Wave 30 in Normal Mode. You don't need to complete the wave, just reach it once. The mode features 2x zombie health, 1.5x zombie speed, and introduces TNT zombies that explode on death. To prepare for Nightmare, ensure you have at least one weapon at level 5+, full armor upgrades, and ideally the Vampire or Regeneration perk for sustain. Most successful Nightmare runs require a minimum of 100,000 coins invested in gear and perks.

Vampire provides 15% lifesteal on all damage dealt, making it ideal for sustained fights and solo play. It synergizes perfectly with high attack speed weapons like the Guitar or Dagger. Critical gives a 25% chance to deal 2x damage, better suited for burst damage builds with weapons like Greatsword or Hammer. For beginners, Vampire is more forgiving as it provides consistent healing. Advanced players often prefer Critical for speedruns and high-wave pushing where maximizing DPS is crucial. Consider your playstyle: defensive players benefit more from Vampire, while aggressive players should choose Critical.

Lucky Spins cost 5,000 coins each and provide random rewards including weapons, perks, traits, and cash multipliers. The drop rates are approximately: 40% cash (2,500-10,000 coins), 30% common perks, 20% weapons, 8% rare traits, and 2% legendary items. They're generally NOT worth it for beginners who should focus on guaranteed upgrades. However, they become valuable after Wave 50+ when you've maxed essential gear and need specific traits like Prodigy or Fortune. Pro tip: Save spins for events when drop rates are boosted by 2x!

The meta 4-player composition consists of: 1 Tank (Hammer + Vampire + Defense trait), 1 Support (Guitar + Team Boost + Medic trait), and 2 DPS (Greatsword/Axe + Critical + Prodigy trait). The tank should focus on grouping zombies and protecting teammates, support maintains buffs and provides emergency healing, while DPS players maximize damage output. For 2-player teams, go with one Vampire sustain build and one Critical burst build. Communication is key - assign quadrants, call out special zombies, and coordinate ultimate abilities for maximum efficiency!

The Untouchable title requires completing Wave 20 in Normal Mode without taking any damage - not even armor damage! The most reliable strategy uses the Scythe weapon for its superior range and i-frames during special attacks. Essential perks include Speed Boost for kiting and Freeze for emergency crowd control. Practice zombie spawn patterns, memorize safe zones on each map, and master animation canceling. Most successful attempts happen on the Factory map due to its predictable spawn points. Expect 50+ attempts before achieving this title - persistence is key!

Dragon is the best first pet investment at 30,000 coins. Its fire breath deals area damage every 8 seconds, perfect for clearing grouped zombies. The Dragon provides 15% damage boost at max level and scales well into late game. Raven offers better single-target damage with its dive attacks but costs 50,000 coins. Wolf is the budget option at 20,000 coins, providing movement speed buffs but lower damage output. For optimal progression: Get Dragon first, upgrade it to level 3, then save for Raven as your second pet. Combine Dragon + Raven for the ultimate pet duo that covers both AOE and single-target scenarios!

Maximize early game earnings with these strategies: 1) Focus on headshot kills for 1.5x coin bonus - aim carefully rather than spray attacks. 2) Activate the Fortune trait if available for 20% bonus coins. 3) Complete waves without deaths for wave completion bonuses. 4) Farm Wave 10-15 repeatedly rather than pushing higher waves with deaths. 5) Join multiplayer games for shared kill bonuses even if you're undergeared. 6) Save Lucky Spins until after Wave 30 when minimum rewards increase. Following this method, you can earn 50,000+ coins in your first hour of gameplay!
