Understanding Game Modes & Mechanics

Hunty Zombie is an anime wave defense experience where you battle zombies across various maps in solo or co-op mode. To win each stage, you must survive and defeat seven zombie waves, with each wave introducing progressively tougher enemies and higher spawn rates. The game scales difficulty based on your chosen mode, affecting both enemy strength and coin rewards.

Core Game Loop

Your primary objective is simple yet challenging: survive waves of zombies while managing your resources and abilities. Each successful wave completion rewards you with coins (also called cash) that fuel your progression through weapon spins, trait upgrades, and pet acquisitions. The amount of coins earned scales with difficulty level and team performance.

ALPHA Release Notice

Hunty Zombie is currently in ALPHA development. Expect bugs, balance changes, and potential data resets. HZ DEV actively updates the game, so strategies and mechanics may evolve. Join their Discord for real-time updates and patch notes.

Difficulty modes determine enemy health, speed, and spawn rates. While specific multipliers aren't officially published, experienced players report that Nightmare mode offers significantly better coin rewards but requires coordinated team play and meta weapons to complete successfully.

Solo vs Team Play Strategy

Hunty Zombie supports solo play or teams up to 6 players maximum. Team composition dramatically impacts your success rate, especially in later waves where coordination becomes essential.

  • Solo Benefits: Complete control, all coins to yourself, no coordination needed
  • Team Benefits: 5% coin boost per friend (max 25%), shared firepower, revival opportunities
  • Optimal Team Size: 4 players - balances firepower with resource availability
  • Friend Bonus: Playing with Roblox friends grants up to 25% bonus coins

Pro tip: Teams smaller than 4 struggle with major zombie waves, while teams larger than 6 face resource scarcity and coordination difficulties. The sweet spot is 4-5 players with complementary weapon loadouts.

Wave Strategy & Enemy Types

To win each stage in Hunty Zombie, you must survive and defeat seven zombie waves. Each wave increases in difficulty with more enemies, faster spawn rates, and special zombie types that require specific strategies to counter effectively.

Enemy Types & Counter Strategies

  • Basic Zombies: Standard undead, low threat individually but dangerous in hordes. Group them for AoE abilities
  • TNT Zombies: Kamikaze attackers that explode on contact. Use dash i-frames or maintain distance
  • Athletic Zombies: Throw basketballs from long range. Priority targets to prevent chip damage accumulation
  • Boss Zombies: Massive health pools, devastating attacks. Coordinate team awakening abilities for quick elimination
  • Special Variants: Watch for glowing zombies and unique models - they often have enhanced stats or abilities

Wave Progression Strategy

Master these wave-specific tactics for consistent success:

  1. Waves 1-3: Conservation phase - use basic attacks, save abilities for emergency
  2. Waves 4-5: Escalation phase - TNT zombies appear, practice dash timing
  3. Wave 6: Preparation phase - clear quickly, position for boss
  4. Wave 7: Boss phase - coordinate team awakenings, maintain spacing
Wave Indicators

Watch for increased spawn rates, screen shaking, and distant roaring sounds. These audio-visual cues signal major waves or boss arrivals. Environmental effects and zombie behavior changes often precede difficulty spikes.

Traits System Breakdown

Weapon Traits are stat modifiers that can transform mediocre weapons into powerhouses:

  • Power I/II/III: +5%/10%/15% Damage - Essential for all builds
  • Agility I/II/III: +2%/4%/6% Attack Speed - Best on fast weapons
  • Focus I/II/III: +5%/10%/15% Skill Damage - Crucial for ability-focused builds
  • Prodigy (Ultimate): +15% Damage, +6% Attack Speed, +15% Skill Damage - The holy grail
  • Fortune: Increased coin drops - Essential for farming runs
Trait Rolling Strategy

Don't waste coins rerolling traits on Common/Uncommon weapons. Save trait spins for Legendary+ weapons. Any trait is better than no trait - even Common traits provide significant stat boosts. Aim for Power III or Focus III on your main weapon before pursuing Prodigy.

Perks System

Six perks currently exist in Hunty Zombie, each offering unique activated abilities:

  • Vampire: Life steal + movement speed - S-tier for survivability
  • Flame: AoE burn damage - Synergizes with Dragon pet
  • DoubleDMG: Temporary damage doubling - New meta perk for bosses
  • Healer: Team healing - Essential for support players
  • Critical: Crit chance boost - Best on high attack speed weapons
  • Shield: Temporary invincibility - Panic button for dangerous situations

Activation: Press E (PC) or Y/Triangle (Console) after charging the perk meter through kills. Time your perk usage for maximum impact - don't waste them on single zombies!
