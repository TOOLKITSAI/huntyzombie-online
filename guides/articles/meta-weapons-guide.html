Reroll Investment Strategy

Smart Rerolling

Expected cost to get Prodigy: ~40,000 Coins (20 rerolls average). However, Power or Focus traits provide 80% of Prodigy's effectiveness at 1/10th the cost. Only chase Prodigy if you have 100K+ Coins saved.

Trait Priority by Weapon Type

  • Fast Attack Weapons (Dagger, Katana): Swift > Power > Focus
  • Heavy Hitters (Greatsword, Hammer): Power > Prodigy > Focus
  • Ability-Focused (Guitar, Scythe): Focus > Prodigy > Power
  • Farming Builds: Fortune > Any combat trait

DPS Calculations & Optimization

Understanding the mathematics behind damage calculation helps you make informed decisions about gear and strategy. Let's break down the DPS formula and optimization techniques.

Base DPS Formula

Total DPS = (Base Damage × Trait Multiplier × Perk Multiplier) × Attack Speed + (Skill Damage × Skill Multiplier × Cooldown Efficiency) + (Pet Damage Contribution) + (Critical Hit Bonus × Critical Rate) Example (Greatsword with Prodigy): Base: 450 × 1.15 × 1.0 × 1.2 = 621 DPS Skills: 1200 × 1.15 × 0.4 = 552 DPS Pet (Dragon): 50 DPS + 20% burn chance Critical: 621 × 2.0 × 0.25 = 310.5 DPS Total: ~1,533.5 DPS (before awakening)

Optimization Techniques

  • Animation Canceling: Cancel recovery frames with abilities for 20% more attacks
  • Ability Weaving: Use abilities between basic attack chains, not during
  • Positioning: Maximize AoE overlap for 3x effective damage
  • Cooldown Management: Never let abilities sit off cooldown for more than 2 seconds
  • Awakening Timing: Use at 60%+ enemy density for maximum value
